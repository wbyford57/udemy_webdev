Disney World Skyliner

Walt Disney World Open Disney Skyliner

Lake Buena Vista, FL September 29, 2019

Disney opened the newest form of transportation today. The Skyliner will make stops at the following ports of call:

  • Pop Century and Art of Animation
  • Carribean Beach Resort
  • Riviera Resort
  • Disney's Hollywood Studio
  • Epcot

Approximate travel times have been revealed:

  1. Pop Century and Art of Animation to Carribean Beach Resort: under 6 minutes
  2. Carribean Beach Resort to Disney's Hollywood Studio: 5-6 minutes
  3. Carribean Beach Resort to Riviera Resort: about 5 minutes
  4. Carribean Beach Resort to Epcot's International Gateway: approximately 15 minutes
